    OUR HISTORY

    Ignited.GG started as a team of six random players grouped together during Elo Hell's Git Gud tournament, known as the Ignited Embers. We have since grown substantially, and are now home to a vibrant community alongside a total three Overwatch rosters and one VALORANT roster.

    OUR STAFF

    OUR PRODUCTIONS

    From the start, our goals included providing exposure for our players, coaching staff, and casting talent. The best way to do so is to put them on-air. Our broadcast talent, both on-camera and behind the scenes, work hard to ensure that our broadcasts maintain a high production value.
