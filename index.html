    OUR HISTORY

    Ignited.gg started as a team of six random players grouped together during Elo Hell's Git Gud tournament, known as the Ignited Embers. We have since grown substantially, and are now home to a vibrant community alongside a total three Overwatch rosters and one VALORANT roster.

    OUR STAFF

    The Ignited.gg Staff is dedicated to keeping the community a clean, safe place for anyone and everyone to enjoy any form of community or Ignited-sponsored activity. They work hard behind the scenes to make sure the server is welcoming to all users for all of their different needs.

    OUR PRODUCTIONS

    From the start, our goals included providing exposure for our players, coaching staff, and casting talent. The best way to do so is to put them on-air. Our broadcast talent, both on-camera and behind the scenes, work hard to ensure that our broadcasts maintain a high production value.

    WHAT WE DO

    COMPETITION

    Competition is in our DNA. Ignited.gg fosters three amateur Overwatch competitive rosters, Ignited Embers, Ignited Kindling, and Ignited SparX, and one VALORANT roster, also known as Ignited Embers. Ignited.gg also hosts monthly tournaments that are produced in-house at https://twitch.tv/ignited_esports. Examples of our recent successes include our GOATS Tournament, Open Role Rumble and the Spooky Showdown.

    COMMUNITY

    Ignited.gg is more than competition. We are a community of gamers that enjoys doing things together. Ignited.GG hosts pick-up games, ranked games, party games, movie nights, and so much more. Learn more by joining our Discord!

    PRODUCTION

    With the help of our production staff, our casters can focus on their work. With the power of our production infrastructure, observers stream their feeds directly to the caster, assuring that casters can catch the action of the match, and our viewers get the highest quality production possible.
