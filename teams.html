    One Final Thank You

    The Ignited.gg team experiment has come and gone, much like the players and staff that made it possible. There are far too many to list here, but we wish to extend all of you a heartfelt thanks for giving it your all and allowing us to be your home for personal and in-game growth. We may have all long moved on, but you will always be a part of the Ignited.gg family. Thank you for playing and making our community feel alive while it lasted.
